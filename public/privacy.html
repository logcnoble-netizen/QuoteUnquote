Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how QuoteUnquote ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit or make a purchase from this website (the "Site"). By using the Site you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order we collect the information you provide, including your name, email address, shipping address, phone number, and the custom text (handle and comment) you submit for printing. Payment card details are collected and processed directly by our payment processor, Stripe — we never receive or store your full card number.

We also automatically collect limited technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages viewed, using cookies and similar technologies to operate the Site and understand usage.

2. How We Use Information

  • To process, print, fulfill, and ship your order through our print and fulfillment partners.
  • To send order confirmations, tracking updates, and customer-service communications.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Sale.
  • To measure advertising performance. We share hashed, pseudonymized identifiers (such as a SHA-256 hash of your email) with advertising platforms including Meta and TikTok via their server-side Conversions APIs to attribute purchases. This data is hashed before transmission.

3. How We Share Information

We share information only with service providers that help us run the business: our payment processor (Stripe), our print-on-demand and fulfillment provider (Printify and its print partners), our hosting provider, and the advertising platforms described above. We do not sell your personal information for money.

4. Custom Text You Submit

Any handle, name, or comment you submit for printing is stored as part of your order record and transmitted to our print partner to produce your garment. Do not submit content you do not have the right to use — see our Terms of Sale.

5. Data Retention

We retain order records for as long as necessary to fulfill orders, comply with legal and tax obligations, and resolve disputes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to opt out of certain advertising-related sharing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. Residents of California, the EEA/UK, and other regions may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, GDPR, or equivalent laws.

7. Children

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

9. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@quoteunquote.example.
