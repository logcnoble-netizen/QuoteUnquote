Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Shipping Policy is provided in the spirit of the U.S. Federal Trade Commission's Mail, Internet, or Telephone Order Merchandise Rule so you know exactly when to expect your order.

1. Production Time (Made On Demand)

Every garment is custom printed on demand. Please allow 3–5 business days for production before shipment tracking updates. Production time is separate from transit time and begins after your payment clears and, for custom designs, after any brief content review.

2. Transit Time

After production, domestic U.S. orders typically arrive within 3–7 business days via standard shipping. Total estimated delivery is therefore approximately 6–12 business days from order to doorstep. International transit times vary by destination and customs.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping is a flat $6.00 for U.S. orders and free on orders of $90.00 or more. Any applicable duties or taxes on international orders are the recipient's responsibility.

4. FTC 30-Day Rule

If we cannot ship your order within the time stated above (or within 30 days if no time is stated), we will notify you, give you the option to consent to a delay, and offer you the choice to cancel your order for a prompt, full refund.

5. Tracking

When your order ships you will receive a tracking number by email, and you can check status anytime using your order token on our Track Order page. Tracking milestones update once the carrier scans your package — allow the full production window before expecting movement.

6. Lost or Delayed Packages

If tracking shows no movement for an extended period or your package is marked delivered but not received, contact support@quoteunquote.example with your order token and we will investigate with the carrier and make it right.

7. Address Accuracy

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We are not able to reroute packages once an order enters production, and orders returned due to an incorrect address may incur a reshipment fee.

This template is provided for implementation convenience and is not legal advice. Confirm your actual production and transit windows with your fulfillment partner and have counsel review before launch.
